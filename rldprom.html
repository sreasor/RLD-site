Welcome to Prom!

April 8th, 2023. 3515 NW 8th Avenue: 7pm

Joytraders

The joytraders are an independent pop punk band based in Gainesville, Florida. With influences from early 2000s alternative and punk music, their sound is something of a cocktail of your favorite emo bands. The flare they bring to the prom lineup is unmatched. Catch them open the show at 8 pm!

Chasing Trends

Hi there, we are Chasing Trends! We are a group that lies in some purgatory between math rock, indie, and jazz fusion (still getting to that one). Our major influences include Delta Sleep, Rush, Hippo Campus, and Faze Wave. Chasing Trends exists based on the idea that American monoculture makes it really easy to be content with what we are given. There is so much out there, and we want you to find it! Catch us perform second at 8:45!

Saturn Lights

Saturn Lights are seasoned veterans of the Gainesville Music scene, with an eccentric sound mashing together pop punk tendencies with the indie music of the Gainesville scene, Saturn Lights have a unique and captivating sound unlike any band from the area. Make sure to catch their set at 9 pm!

Red Letter Day

Red Letter Day is the headlining act for prom 2023. Hailing from Gainesville, Florida, Red Letter Day strives to be the end to your search for new post-hardcore music in the 21st century. Their debut EP, "It Ends Up In Everything", was released in the summer of 2022, giving the band it's first big single, "King John." Since then, the band has been working on recording a litany of new singles, the first of which, "Migraine", was released in March 2023. RLD will be hitting the prom stage at 10pm. The band has been hard at work with the help of their friends to bring you the best house show experience. We present to you: RLD Prom 2023.

Bessie

Bessie is an independent DJ/producer from Gainesville, Florida. Specializing in EDM music, Bessie makes original house music that fits any occasion. Make sure to stick around after Red Letter Day's set to hear some of the coolest beats you have ever heard from RLD's drummer.

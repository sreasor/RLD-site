About

Red Letter Day is a Gainesville-based rock band with members
Eduardo Giralt (vocals), Sam Graf (bass), Sam Reasor (lead guitar),
Daniel Klohr (drums), and Kyle Wampler (rhythm guitar/vocals). Formed
in the summer of 2020, the band set out on a mission to further the
ideas of alternative and post-hardcore music by incorporating sounds
reminiscent of bands like Glassjaw, My Chemical Romance, Radiohead,
Dance Gavin Dance, and Coheed and Cambria. Their latest release,
“It Ends Up In Everything”, is an accurate depiction of the sound the
band wishes to create. Mixed and mastered by Lee Dyess (From First
To Last, Against Me!, Mayday Parade), this record is something that
the band is really proud of, and excited to share with the world. The
band is currently working on some new singles that should be ready for
release late 2022/early 2023. Keep your eyes peeled for new music and
shows near you. Thanks for listening, we love you guys!
